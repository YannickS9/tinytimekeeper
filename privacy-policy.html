Privacy Policy for TinyTime

Last Updated: February 9, 2026

Overview

TinyTime is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains our approach to any personal information that we might collect from you.

Information Collection and Use

TinyTime does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

The app operates entirely locally on your device and does not:

  • Collect any personal data
  • Require user accounts or registration
  • Connect to the internet
  • Track your usage or behavior
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use analytics or tracking services

Local Storage

TinyTime stores only the following preferences locally on your device:

  • Display preferences (show/hide milliseconds)
  • Appearance preference (dark mode setting)
  • Last used timer duration

This data never leaves your device and is stored using standard macOS local storage (UserDefaults). You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the app.

Data Security

Since TinyTime does not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of your data being accessed, stolen, or misused. All functionality operates entirely offline on your local device.

Third-Party Services

TinyTime does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or external libraries that collect data.

Children's Privacy

TinyTime does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
